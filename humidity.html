Analysis: Humidity

In the humidity survey, it is measured as relative percentage to the ratio of highest absolute humidity and current humidity and the current ambient air temperature. Percent humidity was measured in various localities and then plotted against the latitude of the locality. The plot shows that percent humidity is higher between -30° latitude and 40° latitude. These regions are where major rain forests are located, which often see high humidity, mostly in the form of precipitation.
